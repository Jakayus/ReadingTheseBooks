Discipleship Essentials

by Greg Ogden

Physical

Ongoing Workbook

In Brief

Jesus didn't change the world by addressing crowds — he invested deeply in a few. A structured workbook built around that same idea: gather in a small group of two or three, work through 25 weekly units covering the core truths of the Christian faith, and let the process of study and honest conversation shape you into a more grounded follower of Christ.

Joel's Quick Comments

Great workbook for a group of 3 or 4 people looking to get closer in Christ.
