Synopsis

When Cassie discovers a mysterious book that grants the power to travel through any door anywhere in the world, she's pulled into a hidden underground of magical book collectors. As the stakes escalate and the people around her are threatened, she has to navigate dangerous rivals, ancient secrets, and the seductive power of the books themselves — a fast-paced blend of fantasy and thriller built around the irresistible premise of books with magical abilities.
